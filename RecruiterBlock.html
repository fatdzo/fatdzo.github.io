RecruiterBlock

it's an app with the mission to hides those pesky job ads from tech recruiting/staffing companies



Finding a new tech job is getting harder these days because of the increasing number of recruiting companies. They spam the job boards making it hard to spot that dream job you always wanted. If you had bad experiences with recruiting companies, or you generally just don't like them this is the app for you. RecruiterBlock tries to weed out all the job ads from those annoying recruiting companies. For now, this Chrome extension app supports LinkedIn, Indeed, Glassdoor and Dice job boards. Additionally, this app has only been tested with florida's recruiting companies.

This app is absolutely free to use.
